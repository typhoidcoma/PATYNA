
# Patyna — Design Doc

## Overview
Patyna is the visual AI assistant entity for the Luminora system. She appears as a floating mint‑teal butterfly‑core intelligence with a glowing internal core.

Patyna represents:
- clarity
- calm intelligence
- creative momentum

She is not a toy mascot. She is the embodiment of system cognition.

---

## Core Identity

| Attribute | Value |
|---|---|
| Name | Patyna |
| System | Luminora |

| Material | Mint‑teal ceramic / glass hybrid |

---

## Design Goals

- Unique and brandable
- Avoid spherical mascot shapes
- Avoid resemblance to Nintendo‑style characters
- Maintain readability at small UI sizes
- Feel intelligent and calm

---

## Silhouette

Patyna uses a **teardrop / organic blob hybrid**.

Rules:
- wider base than top
- no feet
- no bow
- no perfect sphere
- subtle asymmetry allowed

## Core Feature

### Luminous Core

Patyna contains a glowing internal light representing cognition.

Outer shell: translucent mint glass  
Inner core: luminous white‑mint glow  

The glow pulses when:
- thinking
- responding
- processing tasks

---

## Wings

Butterfly wings represent transformation and intelligence.

Design rules:
- minimal vein detail
- semi‑transparent
- soft glow edges
- readable shape
